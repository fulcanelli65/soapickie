Soapickie™

U.S. Patent Granted – A Safer Way to Pick Up Soap in the Shower

A Real Solution to a Real Safety Problem

Slippery shower floors are a major cause of injuries, especially among older adults and individuals with limited mobility. Bending down in a wet shower is one of the most dangerous movements a person can make.

Soapickie™ eliminates this risk by allowing users to retrieve dropped soap without bending, improving safety, independence, and confidence.

Designed with Dignity and Everyday Usability

Most assistive products look clinical or medical, which can create unnecessary stigma for users. Soapickie™ was conceived with a different philosophy: a clean, friendly, everyday design that blends naturally into any bathroom environment.

  • Modern and approachable
  • Comfortable to hold
  • Neutral and pleasant in color
  • Familiar rather than “medical”
  • Suitable for any home, hotel, or care facility

Soapickie™ is not just a safety tool — it is a product designed to preserve dignity, confidence, and independence.
